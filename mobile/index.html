❓ How to Use Chore Quest
🧒 Managing Kids
➕
Add a kid
Tap the kid picker (top bar) → + Add Kid. Each kid gets their own chores, tokens, streak, badges, and rewards.
✏️
Rename or remove
Tap the kid picker, then tap a name to rename or tap ✕ to remove (requires at least 2 kids).
🎨
Parent theme
Settings → Appearance → Change Theme sets the theme for the active kid. Choose Galaxy, Dino, Princess, Ocean, or Unicorn.
🎨
Kid’s own theme
In Kids Mode, each kid can tap the coloured dot next to their name in the picker to choose their own theme — it overrides the parent theme while Kids Mode is on.
☀️ Chores & Tasks
➕
Add a chore
Tap + Add in any section header. Set name, tokens, difficulty, schedule days, and optional instructions.
✔️
Tick off a chore
Tap the circle on the right to mark done. Tokens and XP are added instantly. Tap ↩ to undo.
📝
Chore instructions
Chores with instructions show a dotted underline. Tap the name to expand them — useful in Kids Mode so kids know exactly what’s expected.
📅
Schedule
Tap day buttons (S M T W T F S) in the edit modal to choose which days the chore appears. Greyed-out chores are not scheduled for today.
⭐
Chore of the Week
Tap ⭐ on a chore to set it as Chore of the Week — earns 2× tokens all week. The gold border highlights it.
⚡
Bonus chores
One-off or extra tasks with no fixed schedule. Great for spontaneous jobs or special efforts.
✨
Custom sections
Settings → Appearance → Add Custom Section creates named groups (e.g. “Homework”, “Pet Care”). Drag ☰ to reorder chores within and between sections.
⭐ Tokens & Rewards
⭐
Earning tokens
Tokens are awarded automatically when chores are ticked. The gold coin shows the running balance. Undo removes them.
🎁
Redeeming rewards
Add rewards in the Rewards section and tap Redeem to spend tokens when earned.
🛍
Reward Shop
Tap 🛍 Shop in the Rewards header to browse suggested rewards. Customise items and costs in Settings → Rewards System → Reward Shop Items.
🎯
Goals & savings lock
Set a savings goal in the Wallet section and link it to a reward. Enable Savings Lock to prevent spending until the goal is reached.
💰
Weekly Allowance
Settings → Rewards System → Weekly Allowance. Set automatic weekly payouts per kid on a chosen day. Each kid can have a different amount and day.
⏳
Token Expiry
Settings → Rewards System → Token Expiry. Enable per kid: tokens not spent within the set number of days get locked (never deleted). A warning badge appears 3 days before expiry. Parents can restore locked tokens at any time.
💪 XP & Levels
📈
XP progress bar
The bar at the top of the Chores tab shows current level and XP. XP is earned by completing chores; undo reduces it. Spending tokens does not reduce XP.
🌱
Levels
🌱 Rookie → ⭐ Helper → 🌟 Star → 🏆 Champion → 👑 Legend → 🔮 Master. Customise thresholds and names in Settings → Rewards System → XP Levels.
🔥 Streaks & Achievements
🔥
Streaks
A streak increases each day at least one Daily Task is completed. Only daily chores count — bonus and custom section chores do not affect the streak. Miss a day and it resets; personal best is saved separately.
🏅
Badges
Tap any badge to see its requirement and earned date. Thresholds are editable in Settings → Rewards System → Achievements & Badges. Raising a threshold removes badges the kid no longer qualifies for.
🔒 Kids Mode, Approval & PIN
🔒
Kids Mode
Settings → Profile & Permissions → Kids Mode. Hides all editing controls. Kids can view chores, tick them off, read instructions, and browse rewards.
✍️
Require Parent Approval
When on, chore ticks in Kids Mode go into a pending queue. Review them via the ⏳ button. Tap ✓ to approve (awards tokens) or ✕ to reject.
📷
Photo proof
When approval mode is on, kids can attach a photo when submitting a chore. Tap 📷 Add Photo in the note modal to use the camera or pick from the gallery. The compressed photo appears as a thumbnail in your approval panel — tap it to view full size. Photos are automatically removed once you approve or reject.
💬
Kid notes
Kids can add a short note (up to 100 characters) when submitting for approval — e.g. “I also wiped the counter”. The note appears in the approval panel with a speech bubble icon.
🔑
Parent PIN
Settings → Profile & Permissions → PIN Management. A 4-digit PIN protects Kids Mode and sensitive settings. Tap the 🔒 lock button to unlock. The PIN syncs across devices via Firebase.
📣
Flash Messages
Settings → Notifications → Flash Message. Send a pop-up message that appears the next time Kids Mode opens. Great for reminders or surprises.
⚙️ Settings Layout
⚙️
Two-level settings
Settings is organised into five categories: Profile & Permissions, Appearance & Sections, Rewards System, Notifications, and Data & Backup. Tap a category to see its options. Tap ‹ to go back; tap ✕ to close settings entirely.
☁ Cloud Sync
☁
Connect Firebase
Settings → Data & Backup → Cloud Database. Follow the 4-step guide to link a free Firebase project (~5 minutes). All data syncs in real time after connecting.
📱
Link devices
Use the same Household ID on every device. Chores, tokens, badges, PIN, and all settings sync instantly across phone, tablet, and desktop.
🔄
Household management
Inside Cloud Database settings: Rename your household, Switch to another, Browse all households in your Firebase, or Delete one permanently. All actions are PIN-protected. Switching to a household with a PIN requires that household’s PIN.
💾 Import & Export
📤
Export options
App Data — all kids and chores as JSON. Connection Details — Firebase config for linking another device (requires PIN; file is AES-encrypted). Export Everything — full backup combining both (also encrypted).
📥
Import options
App Data — restore a JSON backup. Connection Details — switch this device to a different Firebase household (asks for the file’s PIN to decrypt). Import Everything — restore a full encrypted backup including the Firebase connection.
🔒
Encryption
Connection and full-backup exports are encrypted with AES-GCM using your parent PIN as the key. The file contains only opaque ciphertext — no credentials are visible. The recipient must know the PIN to import.
Tip: Tap a chore name with a dotted underline to reveal its instructions. Drag ☰ on any chore or reward to reorder it. Tap a section header to collapse it and save screen space.
📋 Chore Templates

Choose a pack to fill your chore list. You can edit everything after adding.

❓ How it works
✅ Doing your chores
⭕
Tick a chore
Tap the circle on the right of a chore when you’ve done it. You’ll earn tokens and XP straight away!
📝
Read instructions
If a chore name has a dotted underline, tap it to see exactly what to do.
⏳
Waiting for approval
Sometimes a grown-up needs to check your work first. Your chore will show ⏳ while it’s waiting. Once approved, your tokens will arrive!
💬
Add a note or photo
When submitting for approval you can write a short message (like “I also tidied the shelf!”) and attach a photo as proof. Both are optional.
⭐ Tokens & rewards
⭐
Your tokens
The gold number at the top is your token balance. Every chore you finish adds more. Tokens never disappear unless you spend them!
🎁
Spend tokens
Go to the Rewards tab and tap Redeem on a reward you want. Make sure you have enough tokens first.
⚠️
Expiring tokens
If you see a warning badge on some tokens in your Wallet, those tokens will lock soon if you don’t spend them. Locked tokens can be unlocked by a grown-up.
🔥 Streaks & badges
🔥
Keep your streak going
Do at least one daily chore every day and your streak grows. Miss a day and it resets. Try to beat your best!
🏅
Earn badges
Complete chores, build streaks, and earn tokens to unlock badges. Tap any badge on the Stats tab to see how you earned it.
⚡
Level up
The bar at the top of the Chores tab shows your XP. Keep completing chores to fill it up and level up!
