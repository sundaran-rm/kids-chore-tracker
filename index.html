❓ How to Use Chore Quest
🧒 Managing Kids
➕
Add a kid
Tap the kid picker (top bar) → + Add Kid. Each kid gets their own chores, tokens, streak, badges, rewards, challenges, and theme.
📦
Archive a kid
Tap 📦 next to a kid’s name in the picker to archive them. All data is preserved. Archived kids are hidden from chores, overview, leaderboard, allowance, token expiry, and all settings. Restore or permanently delete from Archived Kids at the bottom of the picker. Hidden in Kids Mode.
🏠
Overview tab (2+ kids)
With two or more active kids the 🏠 Overview tab appears — all kids at a glance with today’s progress, balance, streak, and challenges. Tap a card to switch kids.
🎨
Themes & background animations
Settings → Appearance → Change Theme. Ten themes with unique animated particle backgrounds (stars, bubbles, embers, leaves, etc.). Toggle ✨ Background animations in the theme picker to turn them on/off — preference is saved. In Kids Mode each kid picks their own theme from the kid-picker dot.
☀️ Chores & Tasks
➕
Add a chore
Tap + Add in any section header. The modal title says ➕ Add Chore. Set name, tokens, difficulty, schedule days, and optional instructions. Pressing X discards the new chore — it is never created.
✏️
Edit a chore
Tap the chore name or edit button. The modal says ✏️ Edit Chore. X discards all unsaved changes.
✔️
Tick off & undo
Tap the circle to mark done — tokens and XP added instantly. Tap ↩ to undo. Undoing a chore that completed a challenge also reverses the challenge bonus tokens and re-activates the challenge.
📋
Duplicate a chore
Open the chore edit modal and tap 📋 Duplicate. A copy inserts below with “ (copy)” appended and a fresh completion history.
📦
Archive instead of delete
Tap 📦 Archive / Delete in the chore edit modal. Archive hides the chore but keeps history — restore from the 📦 Archived Chores section (above Rewards). Delete is permanent.
📅
Schedule & Vacation
Tap day buttons in the edit modal to choose schedule days. Unchecking all days auto-disables the chore. Use 🏖️ Vacation (next to Save in the Daily Tasks header) to bulk-toggle all chores — Weekdays, Weekends, All, or Pause all. Streaks freeze (not break) when all days are paused.
🚫
Disable a chore
Toggle Disable chore in the edit modal. Disabled and off-day chores are completely hidden in Kids Mode so kids only see today’s active tasks.
📋
Bulk Edit
Tap 📋 in the parent header to open the Bulk Chore Editor. Select chores across all sections and apply tokens, difficulty, schedule days, or enable/disable status all at once. Great for seasonal refreshes. Hidden in Kids Mode.
⭐
Chore of the Week
Tap ⭐ on a chore to set it as Chore of the Week — earns 2× tokens all week. Uses the higher of COTW and any active multiplier day.
⭐ Tokens, Rewards & Gifting
⚡
Token Multiplier Days
Settings → Rewards System → ⚡ Token Multiplier Days. Set 2×, 3×, 5×, or any custom value per day of the week. On those days an ⚡ N× badge appears in every chore section header and the Today progress bar turns golden. The higher of COTW and multiplier day is applied — they do not stack.
💰
Difficulty Pay Scale
Settings → Rewards System → 💰 Difficulty Pay Scale. Set default token values for Easy / Medium / Hard. When you change a chore’s difficulty the token field auto-fills with the pay scale value. Tap Apply to chores to bulk-update all existing chores, scoped to all kids or the active kid.
🎁
Token Gifting
Tap 🎁 Gift in the header to send bonus tokens to a kid with a personal note. Gifts are counted as earned in the Stats charts (not redeemed).
🛍
Rewards & Shop
Tap Redeem to spend tokens. Browse 🛍 Shop for suggested rewards. 💸 Spending History in the Wallet tab shows a timeline of all past redemptions.
💰
Weekly Allowance & Token Expiry
Allowance — automatic payouts per kid on a chosen day. Expiry — unspent tokens lock after a set number of days; a warning badge appears 3 days before. Both only apply to active (non-archived) kids.
🎯
Savings goal
Set a savings goal linked to a reward. Enable Savings Lock to prevent spending until reached.
🎯 Challenges
🎯
Create a challenge
In the Wallet tab, tap 🎯 Create a Challenge (or + Add in the section header). Types: Complete N chores, Reach N-day streak, Earn N tokens, All dailies N days. Set an optional deadline and bonus token reward. Progress counts only from the day the challenge is created.
✏️
Edit a challenge
Tap any challenge card (parent mode) to open the edit modal. Change name, type, target, bonus, deadline, and schedule. X discards all changes. The modal has ✓ Save, 🗑 Delete, and a 🚫 Disable challenge toggle.
🚫
Disable a challenge
Toggle 🚫 Disable challenge in the edit modal. Disabled challenges show a ⏸ Paused badge (only if not yet completed) and award no tokens. Re-enabling resets the start date so only new activity counts.
🔁
Recurring (scheduled) challenges
Set a Recurring schedule in the challenge modal: Weekly (choose a day), Weekends, or Monthly. A 🔁 badge appears on the card. On completion the challenge auto-disables until the next scheduled reset, then re-enables automatically. Deadline bumps forward by one cycle on reset.
📈
Auto-complete & instant updates
When a kid hits the target, bonus tokens are awarded immediately, the token balance and history update in real time, and the challenge card reflects the completed state without any page refresh. One-time challenges auto-disable on completion.
↩
Undo reverses challenge
If a chore is undone on the same day a challenge was completed, the challenge is automatically re-activated and the bonus tokens are removed — for all challenge types including streak challenges.
💪 XP, Levels, Streaks & Badges
📈
XP & level-up celebration
XP is earned from chore completions and never reduced by spending tokens. When a kid levels up a full-screen celebration fires with confetti and sound. Levels: 🌱 Rookie → ⭐ Helper → 🌟 Star → 🏆 Champion → 👑 Legend → 🔮 Master. Customise thresholds in Settings → Rewards System → XP Levels.
🔥
Streaks & vacation freeze
Streak grows each day at least one active Daily Task is completed. Disabled and off-day chores are excluded from the streak calculation. Use Vacation / Pause all to freeze the streak during holidays — it resumes on the next active day. A Streak at Risk alert fires after 7pm when a 3+ day streak is at risk.
🏅
Badges
Unlock automatically for streaks, chore counts, and token milestones. Tap any badge on the Stats tab for requirement and earned date. Create custom badges in Settings → Rewards System → Achievements & Badges.
🏆 Leaderboard & Family Goals
🏆
Sibling Leaderboard
Appears in the Overview tab (2+ active kids). Three modes: ⭐ Tokens (weekly earned), ✅ Chores (weekly done), 🔥 Streak. Resets Monday. The leader gets a 👑 crown. Archived kids are excluded.
🎯
Family Goals
Set a shared token goal in the Wallet tab (2+ active kids). All kids contribute their XP toward a combined target. A progress bar tracks contributions per kid. Achieved goals show a 🏆 banner. Archived kids are excluded from contributions and the 2-kid threshold check.
🔒 Kids Mode, Approval & PIN
🔒
Kids Mode
Hides all editing controls, the archive button, and the bulk editor. Kids see only today’s active, non-disabled chores. Disabled and off-day chores are hidden entirely. Kids can tick chores, view challenges, browse rewards, and pick their own theme.
✍️
Require Parent Approval
Chore ticks go into a pending queue. Review via the ⏳ button — approve (awards tokens + multiplied correctly) or reject. Add a parent note before approving — fires as a Flash Message to the kid.
🔄
Chore Swap Requests
In Kids Mode, tap 🔄 Swap on a daily chore to request a swap with a sibling. Parent reviews via the 🔄 header button.
📣
Flash Messages & Gifts
Tap 📣 to send a mood-tagged message. Gift tokens automatically send a Flash Message. Post-approval parent notes fire as Praise messages.
🔑
Parent PIN
Settings → Profile & Permissions → PIN Management. 4-digit PIN protects Kids Mode and settings. Syncs via Firebase.
📊 Reports & History
📋
Weekly Report Card
Tap 📋 Weekly in the Stats header. Grades S/A/B/C/D by completion %, trend vs last week, best day, badges earned, challenges completed, per-chore heatmap. Tab between kids (2+). 📣 Send to Kid fires a Flash Message. Auto-opens every Monday morning.
📊
Stats charts
7-day and 12-month token charts on the Stats tab. Gifted tokens count as earned (blue) not redeemed (purple). Redemptions are shown separately in purple.
💸
Spending History
The 💸 Spending History section in the Wallet tab shows a timeline of all past reward redemptions — reward name, date, and token cost.
☁ Cloud Sync & Data
☁
Connect Firebase
Settings → Data & Backup → Cloud Database. Link a free Firebase project (~5 min). All data syncs in real time across devices using the same Household ID.
💾
Export & Import
App Data — JSON backup. Connection Details — AES-encrypted Firebase config (requires PIN). Export Everything — combined encrypted backup. Import reverses each option.
Tips: Tap a dotted-underlined chore name to reveal instructions. Drag ☰ to reorder. Tap a section header to collapse it. Press Escape to collapse/expand all. The 📋 Bulk Edit button lets you update many chores in one go. Undoing a chore on the day a challenge was completed also removes the challenge bonus tokens and re-activates the challenge.

❓ How it works
✅ Doing your chores
⭕
Tick a chore
Tap the circle when you’ve finished. You earn tokens and XP straight away!
📝
Read instructions
If a chore name has a dotted underline, tap it to see exactly what to do.
⭐
Chore of the Week
The chore with a gold glow earns 2× tokens this week — do it first!
⚡
Bonus day
When you see an ⚡ N× badge in the chore header it’s a token multiplier day — all chores earn extra tokens today!
⏳
Waiting for approval
Sometimes a grown-up checks your work first. Your chore shows ⏳ while waiting — tokens arrive once approved!
💬
Add a note or photo
When submitting for approval you can write a short message and attach a photo as proof.
🔄
Swap a chore
Tap 🔄 Swap between the difficulty and token badges to offer a daily chore to a sibling. A grown-up will approve or decline.
⭐ Tokens & rewards
⭐
Your token balance
The gold number at the top is your balance. Every chore adds more — tokens only disappear when you spend them or if they expire!
🎁
Spend tokens
Go to the Rewards tab and tap Redeem. Make sure you have enough first!
🎁
Gift tokens
Your grown-up might send you bonus tokens as a gift! Check your Wallet — gifts count toward your total earned.
⚠️
Expiring tokens
A warning badge means some tokens will lock soon. Spend them before they lock!
🎯
Savings goal
Check the Wallet tab for a savings goal — the progress bar shows how close you are!
🎯 Challenges
🎯
What are challenges?
Special goals your grown-up sets — complete chores, earn tokens, or keep a streak. Finish one to earn bonus tokens right away!
📈
Track your progress
Each challenge shows a progress bar that fills up as you complete chores. Progress only counts from the day the challenge was created.
🔁
Recurring challenges
A 🔁 badge means the challenge resets automatically every week or month — a chance to earn bonus tokens again and again!
⏸️
Paused challenges
A ⏸ badge means the challenge is paused — it’s not counting right now. It will come back when your grown-up or the schedule re-enables it.
⏰
Deadlines
Some challenges have a deadline. Days left shows next to the name — finish in time for the bonus!
🔥 Streaks, XP & badges
🔥
Keep your streak going
Do at least one daily chore every day to grow your streak. Miss a day and it resets — but if a grown-up pauses chores for a holiday, your streak freezes safely!
⚡
Level up
The bar at the top shows your XP. Fill it up to level up — from Rookie all the way to Master! Watch for the level-up celebration 🎉
🏅
Earn badges
Build streaks, earn tokens, and complete chores to unlock badges. Tap any badge on the Stats tab to see how you earned it!
🏆 Leaderboard & Family Goal
🏆
Sibling leaderboard
Check the Overview tab to see how you rank against siblings for tokens earned, chores done, or streak length this week. The leader gets a 👑 crown. Resets every Monday!
🎯
Family Goal
Your family might have a shared goal in the Wallet tab. Everyone chips in together — watch the progress bar grow as you all complete chores!
🎨 Themes & personalisation
🎨
Pick your theme
Tap the coloured dot next to your name in the kid picker to choose your own theme — Galaxy, Dino, Princess, Ocean, Unicorn, and more. Your theme has a matching animated background!
📣 Flash messages
📣
Messages from your grown-up
Sometimes a grown-up sends you a message, a gift, or a note after approving your chore — it pops up on your screen. It might be praise, a reminder, or bonus tokens!
